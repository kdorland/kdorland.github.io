## Preparation
Before the lesson, try to figure out the definition of "Cloud Computing". How would you describe cloud computing with a single sentences? Also think about the following:
- Do you use cloud computing for anything?
- What do ordinary people think of when you mention cloud computing to them?
- Are people generally aware of what cloud computing is?
- Why is it named "cloud computing" in the first place?
